When asked to design a website on a topic of her choice, Meghan created Apologetically Unavailable, a tongue-in-cheek apology to everyone whose messages and notifications she's failed to promptly acknowledge. As someone who procrastinates on replying, Meghan uses this website to acknowledge her shortcomings, explain her behaviour, and hopefully earn a chuckle (and yet more forgiveness and patience) from her loved ones.

This project is light-hearted, and this translated into its design being light-coloured and pastel--senimental and silly, with a frivolous curling typeface and various bells and whistles that entertain the site's visitors, while simultaneously fulfilling the assignment criteria for Meghan's university course.
